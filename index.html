The Neon Museum is dedicated to collecting, preserving, studying and exhibiting iconic Las Vegas signs for educational, historic, arts and cultural enrichment. Founded in 1996, the museum has become one of the city's most treasured cultural institutions.

Our collection features more than 250 unrestored signs and 8 restored signs from Las Vegas casinos, hotels, and other businesses. Each sign tells a unique story about the city's vibrant history and the evolution of American commercial art.

Through careful restoration and preservation, we ensure these luminous artifacts continue to shine for generations to come, celebrating the artistry and craftsmanship of vintage neon.
